About

Tony Elison is a pianist and music educator based in Salt Lake City. He has been an active participant in the Salt Lake City music scene for nearly 10 years, and is one of the most sought-after pianists and keyboardists in the area. He can be found performing several times weekly at local establishments in and around the city.

Tony maintains a private teaching practice, and is also faculty at Westminster University and Utah Valley University, teaching contemporary piano playing, musicianship, jazz theory and improvisation, composition and arranging, and contemporary ensemble skills.

For lesson information and business inquiries: tonyelison37@gmail.com.
