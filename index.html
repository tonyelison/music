About

Tony Elison is a pianist and educator based in Salt Lake City. He has been an active participant in the Salt Lake City music scene for nearly ten years, and is one of the most sought-after pianists and keyboardists in the area. He can be found performing several times weekly at local establishments in and around the city.

Tony maintains a private teaching practice, and is also faculty at the University of Utah, Westminster University, and Utah Valley University where he teaches piano, improvisation, composition and arranging, and ensemble skills.
